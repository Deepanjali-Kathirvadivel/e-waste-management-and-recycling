Our Recycling Process

From doorstep collection to certified material recovery — a transparent, sustainable journey

1. Collection

Field staff visit customers, assess e-waste on-site, and record product details with photos and GPS location.

2. Assessment & Quotation

AI-assisted condition scoring and dynamic questionnaires determine reusability. Quotations are submitted for Manager approval.

3. Pickup & Transport

After customer OTP verification, supply chain teams collect devices and transport them securely to the nearest hub.

Sorting & Classification

Smart Segregation at the Hub

At collection hubs, items are classified as Reusable, Repairable, Recyclable, or Scrap based on AI analysis and manual inspection.

  • Data-bearing devices undergo secure wiping
  • Hazardous components are isolated safely
  • Reusable items are refurbished where possible
Dismantling & Recovery

Material Recovery

Non-reusable electronics are dismantled to recover metals, plastics, and rare earth elements through certified preprocessing units.

  • Circuit boards — precious metal extraction
  • Plastics & casings — granulation & reuse
  • Batteries & capacitors — safe disposal

Zero Landfill Commitment

Every device processed through Green Era Recyclers is tracked end-to-end. We maintain full traceability from customer pickup to final material recovery, ensuring compliance with e-waste management rules.
